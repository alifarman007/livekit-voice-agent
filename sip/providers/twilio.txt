  Provider: Twilio Elastic SIP Trunking
  Website:  https://www.twilio.com
  BD +880:  ❌ Not available
  Cost:     US number ~$1.15/mo, inbound ~$0.0085/min

  Step 1: Log into Twilio Console → https://console.twilio.com

  Step 2: Buy a phone number
          Phone Numbers → Manage → Buy a Number
          Select a number with Voice capability

  Step 3: Create SIP Trunk
          Explore Products → Elastic SIP Trunking → Trunks → Create new trunk
          Name: "LiveKit-Agent"

  Step 4: Configure Origination
          Click your trunk → Origination tab → Add new Origination URI
          URI: sip:{{VPS_IP}}:{{SIP_PORT}};transport=udp
          Priority: 10, Weight: 10

  Step 5: Associate Number
          Click your trunk → Numbers tab → Add a Number
          Select your purchased number

  Step 6: Verify Voice Routing
          Phone Numbers → Manage → Active Numbers → Click your number
          Voice Configuration → Should show "SIP Trunk" (not "Webhook")
          If it shows Webhook, change it to your SIP Trunk

  Step 7: Verify Caller ID (Trial accounts ONLY)
          Phone Numbers → Manage → Verified Caller IDs → Add new
          Add the phone number you'll be calling FROM

  ⚠️  Trial accounts: Only verified numbers can call your Twilio number.
      Upgrade to paid account to remove this restriction.
