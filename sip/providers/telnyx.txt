  Provider: Telnyx SIP Trunking
  Website:  https://telnyx.com
  BD +880:  ❌ Not available
  Cost:     US number ~$1/mo, inbound ~$0.003/min (cheaper than Twilio)

  Step 1: Sign up at https://portal.telnyx.com

  Step 2: Buy a phone number
          Numbers → Search & Buy → Select a number with Voice

  Step 3: Create SIP Connection
          Voice → SIP Connections → Create
          Name: "LiveKit-Agent"
          Connection Type: "Credentials" or "IP Authentication"
          For IP Auth: Add your VPS IP {{VPS_IP}}

  Step 4: Configure Outbound
          In your SIP Connection → Outbound tab
          This is for agent making calls (optional)

  Step 5: Configure Inbound
          In your SIP Connection → Inbound tab
          Forward to: sip:{{VPS_IP}}:{{SIP_PORT}};transport=udp

  Step 6: Assign Number to Connection
          Numbers → Your number → Voice Settings
          Connection: Select "LiveKit-Agent"

  Note: Telnyx is often 40-60% cheaper than Twilio for the same
        functionality. Good alternative for US/EU numbers.
