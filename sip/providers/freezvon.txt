  Provider: Freezvon
  Website:  https://freezvon.com
  BD +880:  ✅ Available (Bangladesh virtual numbers)
  Cost:     BD number ~$10-20/mo, free SIP account, setup fee varies

  Step 1: Sign up at https://freezvon.com

  Step 2: Buy a Bangladesh number
          Virtual Numbers → Bangladesh → Select number
          Choose subscription period
          Provide required documents if prompted (ID/passport)
          Complete payment

  Step 3: Get SIP Account
          After activation (within 24 hours):
          A free SIP account is provided with the number
          Go to your dashboard → SIP settings
          Note: SIP server address, username, password

  Step 4: Configure Call Forwarding
          In your Freezvon dashboard → Your number → Forwarding settings
          Set destination: SIP
          Enter SIP URI: sip:{{VPS_IP}}:{{SIP_PORT}}

          Freezvon supports forwarding to:
          - SIP account (free)
          - Phone number (paid per minute)
          - Both simultaneously

  Step 5: Verify
          Call your +880 number
          Check: sip-manage.sh logs follow

  ⚠️  Default: 2 phone lines (concurrent calls). Additional lines ~$5 each.
  ⚠️  Outgoing calls also available via their SIP account.
  ⚠️  Bangladesh numbers may require identity documents.

  Freezvon Support: Live chat on website, Skype, email, or phone.
