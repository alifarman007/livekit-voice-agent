  Provider: Vonage (formerly Nexmo)
  Website:  https://www.vonage.com
  BD +880:  ❌ Not available (outbound to BD supported)
  Cost:     US number ~$1/mo, inbound ~$0.0039/min

  Step 1: Sign up at https://dashboard.nexmo.com

  Step 2: Buy a phone number
          Numbers → Buy Numbers → Select country and features
          Ensure Voice capability is enabled

  Step 3: Create SIP Trunk
          Voice → SIP Trunking → Create trunk
          Name: "LiveKit-Agent"

  Step 4: Configure Origination
          Trunk settings → Origination
          Add URI: sip:{{VPS_IP}}:{{SIP_PORT}};transport=udp

  Step 5: Assign Number
          Associate your purchased number with the SIP trunk

  Step 6: Configure Voice Settings
          Numbers → Your number → Voice settings
          Forward to: SIP trunk "LiveKit-Agent"

  Step 7: Verify
          Call your number
          Check: sip-manage.sh logs follow

  Note: Vonage offers good rates for outbound calls to Bangladesh
        (~$0.04/min) if you need the agent to call BD numbers.
